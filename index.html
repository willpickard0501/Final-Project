Why Weightlifting?

Weight lifting is a form of strength training that involves using resistance, such as free weights or machines, to build muscle strength, endurance, and power. It helps improve bone density, boost metabolism, and enhance overall physical performance, making daily activities easier and reducing the risk of injury. Additionally, weight lifting promotes mental well-being by reducing stress, increasing confidence, and supporting a healthy lifestyle.
